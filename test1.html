What is a SeedFAST?

A SeedFAST is an advanced subscription agreement ("ASA") that allows investors to make an investment now which will convert into shares in the next funding round at a valuation to be determined in that round.

About your investment

You’re investing £20,000 in 3Fin Analytics, which will convert into shares when we do our next funding round.

If we raise at least £650,000 (which excludes any amounts raised before then by way of SeedFASTs, convertible securities or advanced subscriptions) over one or more bona fide funding rounds before 1 April 2024, that will count as a new funding round.

If we have a new funding round or sell the company before 1 April 2024, your investment will convert into shares at a valuation that’s the lower of:

  1. a discount of 10% applied to the pre-money valuation offered to new investors in the new funding round, and
  2. £3,150,000 (so that if we raise at a higher valuation than £3,500,000, then your investment will still convert as if the valuation was £3,150,000).

To protect your investment, in the event that there is no new funding round before 1 April 2024, then on 1 April 2024 (or if the company is liquidated, dissolved, wound up, goes into administration or enters into a scheme of arrangement, prior to such an event) your investment will convert into shares at a valuation of £3,150,000.
